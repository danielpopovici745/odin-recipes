Creamy Hot Cocoa

Description

It's old fashioned, it's comforting, it makes the kitchen smell wonderful and it's good for the soul.

Ingredients

  • ⅓ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • ¾ cup white sugar
  • 1 pinch salt
  • ⅓ cup boiling water
  • 3½ cups milk
  • ¾ teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ½ cup half-and-half cream

Steps

  1. Combine the cocoa, sugar and pinch of salt in a saucepan. Blend in the boiling water. Bring this mixture to an easy boil while you stir.
  2. Simmer and stir for about 2 minutes. Watch that it doesn't scorch.
  3. Stir in 3 1/2 cups of milk and heat until very hot, but do not boil! Remove from heat and add vanilla.
  4. Divide between 4 mugs. Add the cream to the mugs of cocoa to cool it to drinking temperature.
